About Us

Hey, who wouldnt like a good bar hop with friends. Let us find you a drink! Though our founders know their local hot spots, whenever they traveled outside of their city, they had a hard time finding that place that only locals know is special. The problem was that, by the time they find a local whose enthusiasm for a good time matched theirs, the reliability of information would diminish as the night waned. Founded in 2020, Libations was created to solve that specific problem.

Libations will help you locate breweries in the city of your choice. Use the search bar to type in the city you want to search and a list of 5 breweries will appear along with the weather for that day. By clicking on the brewery you desire it will then present you with address information and their website. Click the brewery again and the box will close back. Each city you search for will stay on the page so that you can navigate back to them.
